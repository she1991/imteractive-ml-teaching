What is Interactive Machine Learning?

Machine learning with a human in the learning loop, observing the result of learning and providing input meant to improve the learning outcome. [1]

Lets look at a real-estate pricing model..

Using the crime rates and property size, we have designed a model that predicts the market value of a property.

Influence the model using your human wisdom..

Move around the data points by dragging them to re-train the model to suit your understanding of the real-estate market.

Use the 'Predict' button to check the model against your expectations.

Keep adjusting the model, until it behaves per your expectations.
